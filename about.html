The Origin Story

The Crash

On Feb 29, 2020, a leap year, Steve left DCJS meetup with his three tech acquaintances. After singing Queen Bohemian-Rhapsody, he merged onto the American Legion bridge and got in a rear-ended accident. Little did he know, this kicked off the start of a lonely global pandemic. During this time, he began hiking; he reflected on his work and life purpose for hours. After 83 hikes in 2020 and coming into 2021, he started to feel that familiar hunger for genuine connection.

The Epiphany

March 2020 - Aug 2021 were the most reflective moments in Steve’s life. His entire team got laid off, his dad caught cancer, and he hadn’t met anyone new in years. In September, in a sudden epiphany, he realized what had to be done. He had to bring back the tech community that built him from a college dropout to the software engineer he is today.

The Vision

After closing on a house in the DC suburbs, he did the unthinkable. He decided to start New York Code and Coffee, a community for developers to grow and make friends… 5-hours away from home. Steve’s vision with NYC Code & Coffee is to create a community by software developers for software developers to grow and make friends.
